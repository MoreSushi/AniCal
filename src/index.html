How to Import Your Calendar

Follow these step-by-step instructions to import your generated ICS file into your preferred calendar application

Import to Google Calendar

Step-by-step instructions for importing your anime calendar

  1. 1

    Open Google Calendar in your web browser

  2. 2

    Click the '+' button next to 'Other calendars'

  3. 3

    Select 'Import' from the dropdown menu

  4. 4

    Click 'Select file from your computer'

  5. 5

    Choose your downloaded .ics file

  6. 6

    Select which calendar to add the events to

  7. 7

    Click 'Import' to finish

Import to Outlook

Step-by-step instructions for importing your anime calendar

  1. 1

    Open Outlook (desktop or web version)

  2. 2

    Go to File > Open & Export > Import/Export

  3. 3

    Select 'Import an iCalendar (.ics) or vCalendar file (.vcs)'

  4. 4

    Click 'Next' and browse for your .ics file

  5. 5

    Choose whether to import as a new calendar or add to existing

  6. 6

    Click 'OK' to complete the import

Import to Apple Calendar

Step-by-step instructions for importing your anime calendar

  1. 1

    Double-click the .ics file on your Mac

  2. 2

    Calendar will open automatically

  3. 3

    Choose which calendar to add the events to

  4. 4

    Click 'OK' to import

  5. *

    For iOS: Email the .ics file to yourself and tap it to import

Import to Other Services

Step-by-step instructions for importing your anime calendar

  1. *

    Most calendar applications support .ics files

  2. *

    Look for 'Import', 'Add Calendar', or 'Subscribe' options

  3. *

    Some apps may call it 'Import iCal' or 'Add iCalendar'

  4. *

    Consult your calendar app's help documentation for specific instructions
